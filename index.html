How It Maps to the Patent

  1. Per-head attention observations feed a sink score engine rather than a flat cache policy.
  2. An EMA controller dynamically promotes hot tokens into SRAM and evicts them when they cool.
  3. A mapping table and budget enforce limited SRAM capacity.
  4. The decode path services fast-tier hits with a bypass rather than defaulting to HBM.
  5. The same framework can be extended to per-head slices, multi-tenant quotas, and speculative decode state.
